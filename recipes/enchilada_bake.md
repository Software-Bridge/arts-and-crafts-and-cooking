### Enchilada Bake 1

Enchilada components layered as a bake

Ingredients:
- Corn and possibly flour tortillas
- Enchilada Sauce
- Chicken broth/stock
- Cream of chicken
- Black beans
- Cooked chicken 
- Corn kernels
- Mexican cheese
- Mixed cheddar cheese
- Havarti cheese

1) Mix base sauce, broth and cream of chicken over low heat until blended.
2) Cook and shred chicken, mixing in a couple spoonfuls of sauce from 1.
3) Heat all tortillas on a comal until relatively crisp and cooked, avoid soggy. 
4) Layer in a pan.  Soak tortillas in sauce, layer on beans, corn, and cheese.  Then torillas, chicken and cheese.  Then torillas and all remaining ingredients and cheese.  Top layer tortillas with cheese.
